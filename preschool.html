Play Group Program


Age Group: 2.6 to 3.6 Years

Play Group children enjoy the freedom to choose from various activity materials under the guidance of well-trained and caring teachers. Indoor and outdoor play equipment help them develop physical coordination and gross motor skills. Emphasis is placed on developing communication skills through various thematic activities throughout the year. Children also engage in learning phonics, reading, and writing skills.

Key Areas of Development


  • Communication Skills
  • Recognition Skills
  • Pattern Tracing Skills
  • Self-Help Skills
  • Good Habits
  • Cognitive Skills
  • Application Skills
  • Numeracy & Alphabet
  • Creativity Skills

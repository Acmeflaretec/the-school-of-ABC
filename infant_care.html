Infant care programme


Age Group: 6 Months to 2 Years

Key areas of development are


  • Early Cognitive Development
  • Language Acquisition
  • Socialization Skills
  • Emotional Well-being
  • Fine and Gross Motor Skills
  • Sensorimotor Exploration
  • Establishing Routine and Structure
  • Parental Involvement and Support

Through engaging activities, sensory experiences, and exploration, we awaken your child's innate curiosity and kindle a passion for learning. Our programs offer ample opportunities for your child to interact with peers, nurturing social skills, communication abilities, and emotional growth. At Happy Hearts, Healthy Start, we prioritize your child's emotional well-being within a secure and affectionate setting, enabling them to explore and flourish confidently.

Transition Goals As they grow


  • They explore
  • They learn about themselves
  • They talk and gesture
  • They learn by doing
  • They sort things out
  • They try new stuff
  • They become more aware and coordinated

Our infant program, designed for little ones aged 6 months to 2 years, lays the cornerstone for holistic development. With a keen focus on cognitive, social, emotional, and physical growth, these programs create a nurturing environment essential for the healthy development of our youngest learners.

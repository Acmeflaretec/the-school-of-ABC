UKG Program


Age Group: 4.6 to 5.6 Years

Our UKG program is designed for children aged 4.6 to 5.6 years, aligned with CBSE/ICSE/NEP 2020 standards. We focus on developing fluency in spoken language, reading, writing, math concepts, motor skills, creativity, and general knowledge through interactive digital smart class activities.
